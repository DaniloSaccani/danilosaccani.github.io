Curriculum vitae

Academic CV and summary.

Postdoctoral researcher at EPFL working on learning-based control with provable guarantees, with a focus on model predictive control, safe learning, distributed control, and reliable autonomy.

Current position

  • 2023 – present Postdoctoral Researcher, École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), Lausanne, Switzerland.

Education

  • 2019 – 2023 Ph.D. with honour, Information Technology, Politecnico di Milano.Thesis: Model Predictive Control for Constrained Navigation of Autonomous Vehicles.
  • 2016 – 2019 M.Sc. in Automation and Control Engineering, Politecnico di Milano.
  • 2010 – 2014 B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering, Politecnico di Milano.

Experience

  • 2023 – present Postdoctoral Researcher, EPFL.
  • 2020 – 2022 Teaching Assistant, Politecnico di Milano, for the course Fondamenti di Automatica.
  • 2019 Adjunct Professor, Fondazione Istituto Tecnico Superiore per le Nuove Tecnologie della Vita. Lecturer for a Finite Element Analysis course.
  • 2014 – 2016 R&D Mechanical Engineer, Renova s.r.l. Mechanical design, prototyping, research and development of custom solutions for web tension control systems.

Technical skills

  • Tools: PyTorch, CasADi, cvxpy, Gazebo, Simulink
  • Optimization: convex optimization and nonlinear programming

Teaching and academic service
